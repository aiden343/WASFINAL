Introduction

Drag is a fundamental force that opposes the motion of objects through a fluid, whether it be air or water. In swimming, water drag is a primary factor that limits speed and efficiency, much like wind drag affects the performance of aircraft and vehicles. Both swimmers and aerospace engineers strive to minimize drag to optimize performance.
